Asteroid Mining

What is Asteroid Mining?

Asteroid Mining is the excavation of natural resources from minor planets and asteroids in space. This encompasses the mining of gold, iron, platinum, as well as many other materials.


How does Asteroid mining work?

The specifics of asteroid mining is still unclear. However, asteroid mining will likely use solar powered digging equipment which would be transported by a hydrogen-powered rocket.


Impacts of asteroid mining

The positives:

  • Asteroid mining will increase the global wealth
  • It will help develop space commodities exchange

    • The negatives:

  • It would put many, out of their jobs
  • It can change the orbit of asteroids, and potentially make them land on earth

    • Ethical concerns of asteroid mining

  • Asteroid mining will likely increase inequality
  • Asteroid mining will hurt the economies of developing countries such as South Africa who rely on in-earth mining

    • How can the emerging technology help to reduce the impact of technology on the environment

  • Asteroid mining will help us become more environmentally-friendly on Earth by allowing us to relocate our mining needs
